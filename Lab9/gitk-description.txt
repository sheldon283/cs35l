
1. upper half:
   The upper left of the window shows the history of all commits and the commit messages for
   each one. It is basically a heiarchical branch of all merges and branches.

   The window to the right of this shows the authors of the commit and their email address.

   The window to the right of this shows us the date and  time the commit was made

2. Lower half:
   The lower left side shows us the file changes in diff format. It is basically a ChangeLog
   of all the commits in diff format. It provides us with information about the patch, such
   as comments and lisp/gnus/nnimap.el and we can also see the changes from the old version
   (in red) to the new versions (in green)

   The right window shows us the files that were affected by the selected commit.
